Hello! I'm Sitora

I am Rakhmonova Sitora. I am 21 years old. Currently I'm studying at Westminster International University (direction of economics, 4th year). But at the same time I'm working on my artistic skills.

Having been accepted into Westminster University, I started becoming creative professionally. Around the Westminster University there is an Art Studio where professional teachers teach art. Nowadays, I am one of the active students who attend this Art Club.Apart from the University of Westminster, exhibitions are held annually in the House of Photography, and various open-air exhibitions. I am considered to be the most active participant in every event.

In April 2018, I received a Certificate for participating and for assisting in organizing the Art exhibition Culture Week 2018 exhibition. In March 2018, I received a certificate for participation in the exhibition, which was organized in honor of the Navruz holiday "Navruz Celebration". And also in 2019, in November, I received certificates for participation in exhibitions which were held at the House of Photography “Generation if Independence: My motherland is my inspiration” and on the territory of the University of Westminster “Creative Works of WIUT students”. I am also a participant of the 2nd Tashkent International Biennale of Applied Arts. In 2019, I taught at MasterskayaUz in the Resin Art technique.

I really want to get into CCA LAB Tashkent. I really want to expand my knowledge, gain experience, and share my ideas.

Thanks!
